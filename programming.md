I'm interested in programming (Python, Lean, SageMath, etc) and contributing to open sources. You can find some of my projects and contributions below.

## [Lean](https://lean-lang.org/)

- [Sphere Packing](https://github.com/thefundamentaltheor3m/Sphere-Packing-Lean) (Maintainer, with S. Hariharan, C. Birkbeck, B. Mehta, M. Viazovska)
    - Formalizing Viazovska's proof of the sphere packing problem in dimension 8
    - There was a recent breakthrough by [Math Inc.'s Gauss](https://www.math.inc/gauss), producing `sorry`-free proof of dimension 8 and 24 sphere packings. See [Math Inc.'s press release](https://www.math.inc/sphere-packing), [Lean Community Blog 1](https://leanprover-community.github.io/blog/posts/SpherePacking-1/), [IEEE Spectrum article](https://spectrum.ieee.org/ai-proof-verification), [NYT article](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/06/08/science/ai-scoop-young-mathematicians.html).
- [Mason-Stothers and Polynomial ABC](https://github.com/seewoo5/lean-poly-abc) (with J. Baek)
    - Formalize Mason-Stothers theorem and polynomial ABC conjecture
- [Formal Conjectures](https://github.com/google-deepmind/formal-conjectures/issues?q=author%3Aseewoo5) (Contributor, by Google Deepmind)
- [mathlib4](https://github.com/leanprover-community/mathlib4/issues?q=author%3Aseewoo5) (Contributor, by Lean Community)
- [Lean-GAP (paper)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.02588)
    - Formalizing exercises in Dummit-Foote's Abstract Algebra book
